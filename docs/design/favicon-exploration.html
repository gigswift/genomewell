Chronic Wellness favicon — three variants for review

Each variant is a standalone SVG in public/. The 16 × 16 size is what actually renders in a browser tab — judge there first. The 180 × 180 is the apple-touch-icon size, shown against the real app background #201813.

Variant A

Solid amber badge

Filled amber cw in italic serif on dark Cocoa. Reads loudest at tab size — closest to how Stripe's S or Notion's N announces itself in the tab strip.
